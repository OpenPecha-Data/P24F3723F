ca kuru sārtham

sarveṣāṃ kuśalāni tu sarvārthamanumodya svacaryātrayīṃ ca|buddhatvāya pariṇāmya tatastadanena
puṇyarāśinā bhavatā

janmasvaprameyeṣu devanaralokayoginaśca vaśīkṛtya|duḥkhino'nugrāhyāstvasahāyā avalokiteśvaracaryābhiḥ

caramaṃ bhavaṃ gṛhītvā vyādhijarārāgadveṣāṃśca prahāya|buddhakṣetre bhagavānamitābha iva lokeśo'mitāyurbhava

prajñāśīlatyāgodbhūtavimalakhyātiṃ bhuvi khe svarge ca|loke prasārya śamayan narān yuvatisukharatadevāṃśca
bhūmau

kleśopahatasattvasya jātibhayamaraṇanāśakaṃ jinendrākhyam|lokottaramakliṣṭaṃ śāntābhayākṣayanāmaiva
padamāpnuhi

iti suhṛllekha samāptā||
